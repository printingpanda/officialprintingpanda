If you are like me, you always lose your phone, even when it is in your own pocket or in your hand. I’ve created a product for you: a phone holder that holds your phone on the side of your computer screen and has space for you to charge it and plug in headphones without the worry of it falling off the surface your computer is on. In the process to create my final design, I tinkered with seven different designs. Each one improved upon the three goals I’d set for the final product. They were having the ability to work for most phones and being adapt to newer models not yet available, being most efficient in terms of filament use, and being aesthetically pleasing. I’ve spent the last 3 years perfecting this design to be a product I could put on the market.
